Why Choose SRM Trichy Nursing

SRM Trichy College of Nursing is a member of SRM IST. College of Nursing was started in the year 2018 in the month of October. It is located at SRM Nagar, Near Samayapuram in a spacious and green ambiance with the exclusive building to learn the road spectrum of Nursing Education, Practice & Research. Here students are encouraged to take part in curricular, Co-Curricular & extra-curricular activities.

Courses We Offer & Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Sc. (Nursing)
  • Diploma in General Nursing & Midwifery

The B.Sc. (Nursing) program is designed to provide in-depth knowledge and practical training in nursing, patient care, and healthcare management. It covers key areas like medical-surgical nursing, community health, and anatomy, equipping students with the skills needed for a successful nursing career.

Eligibility Criteria

DGNM: 3 years Programme

Age: 17 year on Dec 31

Qualification: 10+2 with English and must have obtained a minimum of 40% at the qualifying examination and English individually for any recognized board.

Gender: Only Females
